Ques. What is Recycling of elemnts in a Vector?
Ans. When applying an operation to two vectors that requires them to be the same length, R automatically recycles or repeat
     the elements of shorter one,until it is long enough to match the longer Vector.

Ques. Give an example of recycling of elements in a vector.
Ans  c(1,2,3,4,5,6)+c(1,3)
     c
     2,5,4,7,6,9

         OR

     c(1,3)+c(1,2,3,4,5,6)
     c
     2,5,4,7,6,9
